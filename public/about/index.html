About Us

The Open Platform Foundation is a global, open-source initiative created by Android app developers and Android appstores that share vision of making Android developer's life easier. We contribute our time to create tools and standards that simplify supporting multiple appstores. Today, most Android app developers do not publish their apps and games to alternative Android appstores, and thus loose distribution opportunities. We are here to change, create a habit of publishing the apps and updates to all the available appstores. We understand that the developers will only do such if publishing an app to all the appstores is as easy as publishing to one. We want others who share our vision to participate and make it a community project.
