About Us

The One Platform Foundation is a global, open-source initiative created by Android app developers and Android appstores that share a vision of making life easier for Android developers. We contribute our time to create tools and standards that make supporting multiple appstores simpler . Currently, most Android app developers do not publish their apps and games to alternative Android appstores, and this means they lose distribution opportunities. We are here to change that by encouraging developers to publish apps and updates to all available appstores. We understand that developers will only do this if publishing an app to all appstores is as easy as publishing to one. We want others who share our vision to participate and make this a community project.
